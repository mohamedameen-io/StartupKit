# Phase 7: Skills -- AI Skills Matcher

> **Goal:** Evaluate which of the 7 AI Skills from the skills framework fit your business. Each skill can serve as your core offer, a bonus to enhance your offer, or an upsell/add-on. This phase connects the AI tools landscape to the business you've designed.
>
> **Key principle:** Don't sell "AI" -- sell the outcome the AI enables. Clients pay for results (faster proposals, more content, better support), not technology.
>
> **Source framework:** 7 AI Skills Worth Building (skills.md)

---

## 1. AI Skills Scoring Table

> **For each skill, evaluate:** Does this fit the niche, person, and offer I defined in Phases 2-3?
>
> **Fit Score:** Rate 1-10 based on how relevant this skill is to your target client's problems.
> - 1-3 = Not relevant to my niche
> - 4-6 = Somewhat useful, but not a core need
> - 7-10 = Directly solves a pain point my clients have
>
> **Integration Type:**
> - **Core** = This IS your main offer (or the backbone of it)
> - **Bonus** = Included free to make your core offer more valuable
> - **Upsell** = Sold as an add-on after the core purchase

| # | Skill | What It Does | Pricing (Setup + Monthly) | Fit Score (1-10) | Integration Type |
|---|-------|-------------|--------------------------|:----------------:|:----------------:|
| 1 | **Brand Clone** | Creates a consistent AI voice that mirrors the client's tone across all content. Ensures brand consistency without the client writing every word. | $1-2K setup + $200/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
| 2 | **Weekly Autopilot Report** | Generates automated weekly reports pulling data from multiple sources. Saves hours of manual data compilation and formatting. | $1.5-3K setup + $300-500/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
| 3 | **Content Atomizer** | Takes one piece of long-form content (podcast, video, article) and breaks it into multiple platform-specific pieces. One input becomes 10+ outputs. | $2-3.5K setup + $500/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
| 4 | **Proposal Machine** | Generates customized proposals, SOWs, and pitches from templates and client context. Cuts proposal time from hours to minutes. | $1.5-2.5K setup + $300/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
| 5 | **Client Radar** | Monitors client health signals -- engagement drops, sentiment changes, renewal risk. Alerts you before clients churn. | $2-3.5K setup + $400/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
| 6 | **Support Shield** | AI-powered first line of support that handles common questions, routes complex issues, and maintains a knowledge base. Reduces ticket volume by 60-80%. | $3-5K setup + $500/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
| 7 | **Outreach Engine** | Personalized outreach at scale -- researches prospects, writes custom messages, tracks responses, and optimizes based on what works. | $2.5-4K setup + $400/mo | [1-10] | [Core / Bonus / Upsell / Skip] |
